Refund & Return Policy

Order Adjustments

Orders can only be modified within 24 hours after payment confirmation. After this window, production or processing may already be underway, and cancellations will not be accepted. Once production has started, returns and refunds are only possible if the item is defective or does not match the agreed specifications.

Wrong Size or Fit

If the item does not fit as expected, we offer an exchange for the correct size (if available in stock). The customer is responsible for return shipping costs in both directions.

Production & Delivery Timeline

We aim to deliver orders within the estimated timeframe. However, delays may occur due to peak periods or customs processing.

If delivery is delayed:

  • Up to 3 days: 10% refund
  • More than 3 days: 25% refund

Refunds or store credit are issued at our discretion.

A full refund will only be granted if the item meets the return conditions.

Defective Items

If you receive a defective item or an item that does not match your order specifications, you must notify us within 48 hours of delivery. We will offer a full refund or a free replacement at no additional cost if the item meets the return conditions.

Lost or Damaged in Shipping

If your order is lost or damaged during shipping, we will provide a full refund or send a replacement at no additional charge. We handle all courier claims on your behalf.

Cutom / Made-to-order ITEMS

All custom or made-to-order pieces are non-refundable and non-exchangeable, unless there is a confirmed defect or error on our part.

Return Conditions

To be eligible for any refund or exchange, returned item must:

  • Items must not be sale or discounted items, custom/bespoke designs
  • Damaged must not caused due to misuse or improper care
  • Issues must be reported within 48 hours of delivery, with clear photo/video evidence must be provided
  • Items must be unused, unworn, and in original condition (items may be tried on for fitting purposes only and must not be worn outside)
  • Tags must be intact
  • Items must not carry perfume, makeup stains, or any alterations

We reserve the right to reject returns that do not meet these conditions.

Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days and returned to your original payment method.
