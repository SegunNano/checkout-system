Refund & Return Policy

Custom & Made-to-Order Items

All DemiEssence garments are custom-made or made-to-order. Once production begins, we cannot accept returns or issue refunds unless the item is defective or does not match the agreed specifications.

Defective Items

If you receive a defective item or an item that does not match your order specifications, you must notify us within 48 hours of delivery. We will offer a full refund or a free replacement at no additional cost.

Wrong Size or Fit

If the item does not fit as expected, we offer an exchange for the correct size (if available in stock). The customer is responsible for return shipping costs in both directions.

Order Cancellation

  • Before production starts: Full refund available
  • After production starts: 50% refund (to cover material costs)
  • After completion: No refund available

Lost or Damaged in Shipping

If your order is lost or damaged during shipping, we will provide a full refund or send a replacement at no additional charge. We handle all courier claims on your behalf.

Production & Delivery Timeline

Production typically takes 7-10 business days. International shipping takes an additional 3-7 business days depending on your location. Delays may occur during peak seasons or due to customs processing.

Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days and returned to your original payment method.
